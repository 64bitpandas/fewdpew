Interactive

Guilt can come from anything.

To prove to you how easy it is, try out the game below! Write a quick, fun dialogue about a random icon to make your friends feel really guilty.
Here are a few examples: (It's really easy)

Text from Uncle Richard

Richard: Happy holidays! I hope you like the sweater vest I got you for Christmas! I spent a month making it.

That Annoying Update Popup

Friend: Should I update this software?
You: Nah, it's fine. I never update mine.
2 weeks later- Friend: My computer is broken and all my files are gone :(

Picture

Bill gets an embarrassing picture of Bob.
Billy: Bill, you should send that picture to everyone!
You are unsure, but do nothing to stop it.
Three weeks later, Bob is found dead. It was a suicide.
